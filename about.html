About Football Agency SL

History

The history of football agency in Sierra Leone is a recent, developing story, moving from informal arrangements to formal structures, marked by the establishment of the Football Agents Sierra Leone (FASL) and the Sierra Leone Football Association (SLFA)'s formal certification programs in early 2025, aiming to professionalize player representation and facilitate international moves, with key figures like Chernor Musa Jalloh and Ishiaka Koroma leading the charge to build pathways for players like Abdulai Juma Bah to reach European leagues, a stark contrast to the pre-war era of less structured management. .


Mission

To provide professional pathways for local athletes, bridging the gap between local leagues and international opportunities.


Vision

To become West Africa's leading football agency, setting the standard for talent development and management.
